molecool.measure.calculate_center_of_mass(symbols, coordinates)[source]¶

Calculate the center of mass of a molecule.

The center of mass is weighted by each atom’s weight.

Parameters

  • symbols (list) – A list of elements for the molecule

  • coordinates (np.ndarray, or list-like) – The coordinates of the molecule.

Returns

center_of_mass – The center of mass of the molecule.

Return type

np.ndarray

Notes

The center of mass is calculated with the formula

\[\vec{R}=\frac{1}{M} \sum_{i=1}^{n} m_{i}\vec{r_{}i}\]
